CONFERENCE BREAKS

Attending conferences can be stressful.
Sitting in conference meetings for hours,
attendees usually feel stressed, stiff and out of sorts.

BENEFIT OF CONFERENCE BREAKS

Take the edge off by offering your attendees a Stretch and Energize session. These sessions are scheduled in between conference sessions and last between 15-50 minutes. Participants learn simple stretches and breathing techniques which are done while sitting and standing, while dressed in work attire. Attendeees will:
  • re-energize
  • Destress
  • Be more focused
  • Combat conference burnout

STRETCHES AND BREATHING TECHNIQUES:

  • reduce tension in shoulders, back, neck, hips and legs
  • bring flexibility to the spine
  • improve circulation and digestion
No matter what your age, body type or physical ability - everyone can do these stretches. All poses are done in a chair or standing while wearing work clothes.
