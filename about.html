ABOUT KARIN


Karin Wiedemann has more than 25 years of experience as a leadership and career coach, trainer and consultant in both the public and private sectors in the U.S. and Europe. A graduate of Georgetown University's Leadership Coaching Certificate Program, and an International Coach Federation (ICF) member. Karin provides leadership coaching to professionals seeking to make significant career and life changes. Her clients are professionals from the public and private sectors in both the U.S. and Europe. They include International Monetary Fund, The World Bank Group, U.S. Treasury Department, Princeton University, Crowell Moring LLC, Womble Carlyle LLC, U.S. National Park Service, Walter Reed Army Medical Center, Service Employees International Union and National Center for International Visitors.
