COACHING

Are you eager to make a change but feeling stuck?
Where might you want to go next?

KARIN CAN HELP YOU TO:

  • Dig deeper to identify the job and lifestyle you want next
  • Develop a Road Map
  • Stay open to possibilities, maintain a positive attitude
  • Set realistic goals and stay motivated
  • Develop a Networking Strategy, build new relationships, nurture previous ones

Karin's approach to coaching is to ask powerful questions, hear what you are unable to hear, and help you build a solid action plan and set goals. Karin guides her clients as they take action and holds them accountable to their goals. Most of Karin's coaching sessions take place over the phone. She coaches clients no matter where they are locaed.
